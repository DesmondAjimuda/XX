About the CEO

Oyindamola Akinyemi, the CEO of Honey Home of Luxuries, has always had a passion for design. From a young age, she was fascinated by the way that colors, textures, and patterns could be combined to create beautiful spaces. Oyindamola founded in 2017, and she has been transforming homes and commercial spaces.

With a keen eye for detail and a dedication to quality, Oyindamola has become a leading figure in the interior design industry. She takes a hands-on approach to every project, collaborating closely with her clients to ensure that their vision is brought to life.

Along with her team of talented designers, Oyindamola is committed to delivering exceptional service and creating spaces that her clients will love for years to come. Her passion for design and commitment to excellence are evident in every project she undertakes, making her a sought-after designer and a valued member of the interior design community.
